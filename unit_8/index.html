bio

I'm a NY-based 3D artist, graphic designer and web producer with 5 years of industry experience. I specialize in photoreal 3D modeling, texturing and graphic design but have worn many hats over the years. I completed Masters of Science degree program in Digital Imaging and Design, New York University and was a recipient of "Digital Imaging and Design Award for Excellence". I graduated Volgograd State Pedagogical University, Russia, with a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science, summa cum laude, in 2010. I received a Silver Award from The American Design Awards for website design in January 2010. Thanks for visiting and checking out my work.
